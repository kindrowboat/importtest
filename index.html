What is going on?

piepi is a Raspbery Pi physically located in Toronto, Canada. It is also a tilde server meant for creating and sharing (optionally) pi/e related artwork.

The homepages of piepe's users will be currated into pi/e zine on a rolling basis. Users maintain the full rights to their work. This page and the zine are published under the CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license.

How can I participate?

If you're interested in getting a homepage and shell access to make art, email "kindrobot AT tilde DOT team" with the following information:
  1. your prefered username
  2. what you want to do on pipie
  3. your public key (if you have one)
  4. (optional) your mailing address if you want a physical copy of the zine
